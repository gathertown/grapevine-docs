The HubSpot connector indexes CRM data including contacts, companies, deals, and tickets.

## What gets indexed

- Contacts
- Companies
- Deals
- Tickets
- Notes and activities
- Custom properties

## Setup

### 1. Create a private app

1. In HubSpot, go to Settings > Integrations > Private Apps
2. Click "Create a private app"
3. Name your app "Grapevine"
4. Add scopes:
   - `crm.objects.contacts.read`
   - `crm.objects.companies.read`
   - `crm.objects.deals.read`
   - `tickets`

### 2. Generate access token

1. Click "Create app"
2. Copy the access token

### 3. Connect to Grapevine

1. In Grapevine, go to Integrations
2. Click "Connect" on HubSpot
3. Enter your access token
4. Click "Save"

## Permissions

HubSpot permissions are preserved:

- Users only see records they have access to
- Team permissions are respected
- Private records remain private

## Webhook setup

For real-time updates:

1. In HubSpot Settings > Integrations > Webhooks
2. Create webhook subscriptions for:
   - `contact.creation`
   - `contact.propertyChange`
   - `company.creation`
   - `company.propertyChange`
   - `deal.creation`
   - `deal.propertyChange`
3. Set target URL to: `https://webhooks.grapevine.ai/hubspot`

## Indexed properties

Standard properties:
- Name, email, phone
- Company name, domain
- Deal amount, stage
- Ticket status, priority

Custom properties are also indexed.

## Limitations

- Maximum 1,000,000 records per object type
- Deleted records are removed from index
- Some custom property types may have limited support
- Attachments larger than 10MB are not indexed
